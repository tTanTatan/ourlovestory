Hola!! My Brave Lion

Our love story begins with a single "Hey" followed by "Have You Ever Tried", a lyrics from the song Maybe the Night.
You responded and complied despite how corny and embarrassing it sounded. I actually never expected that you will
reply since I'm just someone who secretly admires you from afar. After a long conversation, I feel something in my
stomach. (tae ata, jk) Something unexplainable, it feels like some sort of butterfly, something I never thought I
would experience again. My world suddenly turns upside down when I met you. Everything changed, my mindset, my hope,
and my dreams. You lift me up and bring light in my dark and misserable life. You bring joy, excitement, and kilig
everytime. Because of you, my optimism was revived, and I once again began to believe in love. You are the cause
under my charming smiles. As soon as I met you, everything started to go well. This is where everything
between us started, where our love story begins, a story that will never end.
